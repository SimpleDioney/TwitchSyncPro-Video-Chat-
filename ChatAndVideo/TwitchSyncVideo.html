How to get your Twitch OAuth Token:

  1. Visit the Twitch Chat OAuth Password Generator
  2. Click on "Connect with Twitch" and log in to your Twitch account
  3. Authorize the application to generate your OAuth token
  4. Copy the generated OAuth token (including the "oauth:" prefix)
  5. Paste the token into the "OAuth Token" field below
